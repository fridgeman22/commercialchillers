📬 How Enquiries Work

When someone fills in the enquiry form on the website, their details are automatically sent to your email inbox as a pre-formatted message. You'll see their name, business, phone number, equipment needed and any notes.

To change the email address enquiries go to, update it in the Contact Details section.

💡 Tips for Converting Leads
  • Call back within 2 hours — response time is your biggest advantage over big suppliers
  • Ask about their timeline and budget early — qualifies the lead fast
  • If they're not ready to buy, offer to send a brochure — keeps you top of mind
  • Always ask "is there anything else you need to quote?" — upsell opportunity
